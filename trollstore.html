TrollStore

TrollStore is an app sideloader for iOS 14 - 16.6. & 17.0 that bypasses codesigning under specific circumstances.

This allows for unlimited applications, no revokes, JIT & arbitary entitlements.

If you are on iOS 14.0.1 beta or earlier, or is running 16.7.x (excluding 16.7 RC (20H18)) or on iOS 17.0.1 or above, you will never be supported by TrollStore.

Finding your iOS version

  1. Open the “Settings” application
  2. In the app, find “General”, then “About”, “Software Version”. Beside it should be your iOS version.
