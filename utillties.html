Utillties

These tools are helpful or may improve your experience of sideloading. You may want to install one of these!

Version Table

From To All devices Information
iOS 15 iOS 16.1.2 Cowabunga Removes any blacklist from enterprise certificates, disable three app limit, and many more features.
iOS 17.0 iOS 18.0.1 BlacklistBeGone Removes any blacklist from enterprise certificates. Requires you to disable Find My.
iOS 16.0 iOS 18.0 RC / 18.1 beta 4 SparseBox Removes the three app limit on free developer accounts. Requires you to disable Find My. Does not include version 17.7.1.
iOS 15 ~ LiveContainer An app launcher. Very useful for free developer certificate users, as it can launch instances of an application without restrictions applying. This is not an emulator or a hypervior.

Finding your iOS version

  1. Open the “Settings” application
  2. In the app, find “General”, then “About”, “Software Version”. Beside it should be your iOS version.
