Alternative Methods

Below shows a table of signers that do not require a computer.

Majority of these signers support the latest firmware versions.

These signing methods may not be perfectly reliable. Some certificates may be revoked. Use at your own risk. View more information here.

General Certificate Signing

From To All devices
iOS 11 ~ ESign
iOS 11 ~ Feather
iOS 14 ~ FlekSt0re
iOS 11 ~ AppDB

Developer Certificate Signing

All these listed methods below require a payment, minimum $4 (amount varies by region). Developer certificates are extremely popular on latest firmwares.
From To All devices
iOS 14 ~ KravaSign
iOS 11 ~ Signulous
iOS 14 ~ DXSign

Enterprise Signing

Enterpise Certificate signing is extremely unreliable. Use at your own risk. Revokes will occur constantly.
From To All devices
iOS 14 ~ Scarlet
iOS 11 ~ ESign (Khoindvn)

Finding your iOS version

  1. Open the “Settings” application
  2. In the app, find “General”, then “About”, “Software Version”. Beside it should be your iOS version.
To obtain an enterprise certificate, follow this tutorial on our website to find official sources. Revokes are not guarenteed.
