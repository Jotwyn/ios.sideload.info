iOS 4

Below is the supported upgrade and downgrade information for iOS 4 on Apple devices.

From To ARM64 ARM64e
iOS 3.1.3 iOS 4.2.1 No No
iOS 4.0 iOS 5.1.1 No No

How to check your iOS version

Follow these steps to find out your current iOS version:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Navigate to General > About.
  3. Look for the line that says Software Version.
