Finding your iOS version

  1. Open the “Settings” application
  2. In the app, find “General”, then “About”, “Software Version”. Beside it should be your iOS version.

Finding your device architecture (arm64, etc)

  1. Type in your device model name. You can locate your device model in "General", "About", and then next to "Model Name".
