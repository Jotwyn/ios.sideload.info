iOS 15 Sideloading & Jailbreak Methods

Last updated: June 2024

  • Jailbreak: Available for many devices (see below).
  • TrollStore: Fully supported on iOS 15.x.

AltStore

Follow the same steps as for iOS 18 above. AltStore works on iOS 15.

SideStore

Follow the same steps as for iOS 18 above. SideStore works on iOS 15.

Sideloadly

Follow the same steps as for iOS 18 above. Sideloadly works on iOS 15.

eSigner Apps

See the eSigner Apps section above. These work on iOS 15, but certificate revokes are common.

TrollStore (iOS 15.x)

  1. Check your iOS version in Settings > General > About. TrollStore works on all iOS 15.x versions.
  2. Visit TrollStore for the latest instructions and supported devices.
  3. Download the appropriate installer (e.g., TrollInstallerX for A12+ devices).
  4. Follow the step-by-step guide on the TrollStore website to install and activate TrollStore.
  5. Once installed, open TrollStore and import your .ipa files for permanent installation.

Jailbreaking (iOS 15)

  1. Check The Apple Wiki Jailbreak Status for your device and iOS version.
  2. palera1n supports A8–A11 devices (iPhone X and below) on iOS 15.x. See palera1n website for instructions.
  3. Fugu15 Max supports some A12+ devices on iOS 15.0–15.4.1. See Fugu15 Max GitHub for details and instructions.
  4. Other jailbreaks may be available for specific devices/versions. Always check the latest status and guides.

Xcode (macOS only)

Follow the same steps as for iOS 18 above. Xcode sideloading works on iOS 15.
