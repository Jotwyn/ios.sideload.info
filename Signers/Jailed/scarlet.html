Scarlet

Scarlet is a sideloader on iOS that uses enterprise certificates. Alternatively, you can also sign Scarlet using your own certificate.

Scarlet is highly deprecated and is not recommended for use. If signing Scarlet using your own certificate, we recommend using Feather or ESign.

Prerequisites

  • An iPad or iPhone running iOS 14+. (could work on lower versions, unsure.)
  • Your certificate (optional if using direct install).

    • Direct Installing Scarlet

    If you would like to sign Scarlet using your own certificate, keep scrolling.

    1. Open Safari on your iDevice.
    2. Visit usescarlet.com.
    3. Scroll to the bottom and click Install. If any ads redirect you, close the ad and continue.
    4. Select "Direct Install".
    5. After 10 seconds, you should be prompted to install Scarlet. Tap "Install".

    Verifying Application

    1. Open Settings.
    2. Go to General.
    3. Scroll to VPN & Device Management.
    4. Open it and you will find a developer application.
    5. Click on it. Once opened, click "Verify App".

    Signing Scarlet using your own certificate

    Downloading Scarlet

    1. Open Safari on your iDevice.
    2. Visit usescarlet.com.
    3. Scroll to the bottom and click Install. If any ads redirect you, close the ad and continue.
    4. Select "IPA(Custom Cert + Developer Login)".
    5. After 10 seconds, your .ipa file should be installed.

    Signing Scarlet

    1. Open Safari on your iDevice.
    2. Open kravasigner.com. Alternatively, you can use other web signers.
    3. Insert the required files. (self explanatory)
    4. Tap Upload & Sign
    5. Tap Install IPA
    6. Wait until the application is on your home screen.

    Verifying Application

    1. Open Settings.
    2. Go to General.
    3. Scroll to VPN & Device Management.
    4. Open it and you will find a developer application.
    5. Click on it. Once opened, click "Verify App".

    Importing your certs

    1. Install any application. Preferably, it should be the one you are gonna install.
    2. While installing, a white screen should appear and will have two buttons for "Import Cert" & "Export Account".
    3. Tap Import Cert. Import the certificate that you used to sign Scarlet.
    4. Once complete, Scarlet should prompt to install an application. Tap "Install".
