Get Started

ESign

ESign is an application signer that allows you to use your own certificate

Prerequisites

  • iPad or iPhone TV
  • A working Apple ID (use a burner) - Only on iOS 17.6+
  • A working certificate

    • • As of right now (August 28th), Appdb does not allow enterprise signing.

Please select the OS you are using.

Installing Appdb

  1. Visit appdb.to on your device.
  2. At the top of the page, tap "Install appdb"
  3. Enter your email address.

    4. • Do not use an Apple registered email address (@me.com, @icloud.com, @mac.com).

  4. Once linked, a configuration profile should be prompted to be installed. Tap Allow.
  5. Open the Settings app. Navigate to General > VPN & Device Management. Tap "Install appdb".
  6. At the top right, tap "Install". Type in your passcode. Then, tap "Install" again.
  7. Read your screen & accept the TOS. Tap "Next" to accept on your top right. Finally, click "Install".

Connecting your Apple ID

  1. A new bookmark should appear on your device. It should be named "appdb". Open it.
  2. At the top of the page, tap "Selected Device". Find the option "Install apps with free Apple Developer account" and tap it.
  3. Enter your credentials and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Once complete, you should be connected using your Apple ID.
  5. Now, you can install apps. At the top, you can select a handful of Appdb picked apps, or import your own .ipa file.

    6. Import a custom .ipa by tapping "Selected Devices", then "My apps (ipa files)".

Linking your Apple TV

  1. On any device, open appdb.to.
  2. Enter your email.
  3. Follow the instructions on the screen.
