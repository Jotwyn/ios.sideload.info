TrollHelperOTA

TrollHelperOTA is a type of installation vector that installs TrollStore on your device.

Prerequisites

  • An iPad or iPhone running iOS 14 - 15.6.1 & 16.0 b1 - b4

Installing TrollHelper

  1. Open Safari on your iDevice.
  3. Click this URl.
  4. If unable to access, type: itms-services://?action=download-manifest&url=https://jailbreaks.app/cdn/plists/TrollHelper.plist
  5. Click install.

Installing Trollstore

  1. Open the app "GTA Car Tracker" on your home screen.
  2. Once opened, click "Install TrollStore.
  3. Your device should respring, and TrollStore should be installed on your device.
