TrollInstallerMDC

TrollInstallerMDC is a type of installation vector that installs TrollStore on your device.

TrollStore is an app-permasigner that bypasses Apple's regular restrictions due to a coretrust (codesigning) exploit. Therefore, it allows unlimited apps, no revokes, JIT, arbitary entitlements, and much more.

TrollStore is not a jailbreak.

Prerequisites

  • An iPad or iPhone running iOS 15 - 15.7.1 or 16.0 - 16.1.2
  • Latest version of Sideloadly
  • Latest version of iTunes (not from the Microsoft store)
  • Latest version of iCloud (not from the Microsoft store)
  • • If on MacOS, you do not need to install iTunes or iCloud.
  • Latest version of TrollInstallerMDC
  • Computer running Windows 10+ or MacOS 10.12+
  • Tips app installed on your device

Installing TrollInstallerX

  1. Open the Sideloadly application. If iCloud or iTunes are not downloaded, do so now.
  2. Plug your iOS device into your computer. Ensure your iDevice is trusted.
  3. Drag and drop, or select the
    TrollInstallerMDC.ipa
    .ipa file.
  4. Enter your Apple ID and password. Your credentials will only be sent to Apple for verification.

Enabling Developer Mode

Starting iOS/iPadOS 16+, all users are required to enable developer mode to install developer apps.

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Scroll down to Privacy & Security.
  3. On Privacy & Security, scroll down to Developer Mode. Click enable.
  4. Your device may restart. Once enabled, proceed with the next steps.
