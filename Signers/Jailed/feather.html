Get Started

Feather

Feather is an application signer that allows you to use your own certificate

Before proceeding, you must obtain a certificate that comes in 2 files (when decompressed); .p12 & .mobileprovision. To learn how to get a certificate, visit this guide.

Prerequisites

  • iPad or iPhone TV running iOS 14 or above
  • Latest version of Feather
  • Your certificate (.p12, .mobileprovision & password (if applicable)

Installing Feather

  1. Open sign.kravasign.com
  2. Insert the required files. (self explanatory)
  3. Tap Upload & Sign
  4. Tap Install IPA
  5. Wait until the application is on your home screen.

Verifying Application

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to General.
  3. Scroll to VPN & Device Management.
  4. Open it and you will find a developer application.
  5. Click on it. Once opened, click "Verify App".

Adding our Certificate

    • We must add our certificate to Feather in order sign other applications.

  1. Open the Feather application.
  2. At the bottom, navigate to the Settings section.
  3. Tap Add Certificate > Add Certificates.
  4. Follow the instructions on your screen. Import each file one by one.
  5. • Your certificate should have been successfully added, and we can now sign apps.

Signing your first app

    • Before proceeding, please find your .ipa file you want to install.

  1. Navigate to the Library secrion at the bottom of your screen.
  2. At the top, click the button that says Import.
  3. Click Import from Files at the bottom. Alternatively, you can insert a direct URL to the download file.
  4. Once your .ipa has been imported, click Sign (app) at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Click Start Signing at the bottom.

      6. Online Signing Mode (iOS 17.6.1+)

        • This step is only optional for users running iOS 17.6 & lower.

      1. Navigate to the Settings tab at the bottom of your screen.
      2. Under Signing Server, select "Online Install Method". Ensure it is toggled to the right.
