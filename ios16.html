Finding your iOS version

  1. Open the “Settings” application
  2. In the app, find “General”, then “About”, “Software Version”. Beside it should be your iOS version.

ARM64 or ARM64e

    ARM64 devices are A11 and below which are: iPhone X, iPad 7th gen, iPad Air 2nd gen, iPad Pro 2nd gen, iPhone SE 1st gen and below.

    ARM64e devices are A12 & M1 and above which are: iPhone XR, XS & XS Max, iPad 8th Gen, iPad Air 3rd gen, iPad Pro 3rd gen and above, iPhone SE 2nd gen and above.

    An easy to find out is by visting ios.cfw.guide. Select your device and it will show you your architecture and bionic chip.
